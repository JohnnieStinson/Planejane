Frequently Asked Questions

How do I submit my music?

Use the contact form above and select "Submit Music" as the subject. Include a link to your project (SoundCloud, DatPiff, etc.) and a short bio in the message field. We review all submissions but can only feature a limited number each week.

How long does it take to get a response?

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 1–2 business days. Music submissions may take up to 5 business days as our team carefully reviews each project.

Can I advertise on PlaneJane?

Yes! We offer banner ads, sponsored content, and newsletter placements. Select "Advertising & Partnerships" in the form above and we'll send you our media kit with rates and reach statistics.

Is PlaneJane free to use?

Yes, PlaneJane is completely free for listeners. You can stream and download all available content without a subscription. Artists pay nothing to have their music featured.
